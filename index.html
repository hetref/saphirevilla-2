About Sapphire Villa

BBMP Approved - PRM/KA/RERA/1251/446/PR/130924/007016

Sapphire Villas is a premium residential enclave located in the serene neighborhood of Sadarmangala, Whitefield, Bangalore. This exclusive development features a limited collection of luxurious 4 BHK bespoke villas (3420 - 4810 sq. ft.), designed to reflect timeless elegance and modern sophistication.

Each villa is a masterpiece of architectural brilliance, offering spacious living, abundant natural light, and high-end finishes—all in a tranquil setting just minutes from Whitefield, ITPL, ORR, and other key tech and lifestyle hubs.

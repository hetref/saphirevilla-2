About Between The Greens

BDA Approved. Project RERA - PRM/KA/RERA/1251/308/PR/120225/007494

Between The Greens is a premium villa project by 42 Estates, located in the peaceful enclave of
Chikkanekkundi, off Whitefield–Varthur Road. Spread across 9.5 acres, it features 147 luxurious 4 BHK villas (3,700–4,800 sq ft) designed to blend nature and elegance.

Enjoy world-class amenities like a clubhouse, pool, gym, sports courts, gardens, and more—all just minutes from Whitefield, Sarjapur, and ORR.

Possession begins December 2029, with prices from ₹4.2 Cr.
Presented by Green Shoots, authorized channel partner of 42 Estates.
